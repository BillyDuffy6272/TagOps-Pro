# 01 - Defining the Project w/ Boundaries

## **Tracking Organisation + Operational Tool for Business Owners**

### Problem Statement

---

Business owners who use Google Tag Manager and Google Ads can at times find it confusing as to what Tags are installed, what aren’t and what is actually operational and necessary. This can waste resources when attempting to fix this and can confuse business owners who have little to no experience with dealing with this complicated software, as it is often set up by third party contractors. With a software in place to help better organise this business owners will have more clarity with their tracking set up so don’t have to waste resources fixing an issue which may not even exist. This will also allow them to implement tracking without using the dedicated Google softwares that are complicated to use, and allow business owners to verify that the tracking installed is running and operational. By Tracking using these softwares I am referring to businesses setting up software that tracks and measures the success of their website. This includes recording data as to how many people complete a form or press on a email/phone. This is necessary information for businesses so they can see how well their website is converting and performing so they can maximise their conversions.
